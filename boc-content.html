ABOUT ASIA BOC

The Independent Authority

ASIA Board of Certification (ASIA BOC) merupakan badan independen di bawah Association of Asia Pacific Academician (ASIA) yang bertanggung jawab menetapkan standar kompetensi, skema sertifikasi, tata kelola asesor, pengendalian mutu, registrasi profesional, dan pengembangan sistem sertifikasi internasional.

Vision

To become the leading certification governance body in Asia Pacific that promotes internationally recognized professional competency standards.

Mission

  • Develop Professional Standards
  • Develop Certification Schemes
  • Maintain Certification Quality
  • Manage Professional Registry
  • Ensure Global Recognition
